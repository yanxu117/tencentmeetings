## IELTS Part 3 Strategy

### Key Tips Mentioned:
1. **Don't use personal examples** - Use general examples instead
2. **Immediate response** - Answer within 2 seconds of question
3. **Continuous speaking** - Keep the conversation flowing
4. **Show concepts clearly** - Explain the concept, don't let them guess
5. **React to questions** - Understand the examiner's question, not just isolated words
6. **Use advanced vocabulary** - Demonstrate higher-level words for better scores

### Common Question Types:
- "What do you think?"
- "Do you agree/disagree?"
- "Why do you think...?"
- "What's the difference between...?"
